About the Pet Collector

I have had animals since I was five years old. Growing up we hand raised parakeets, with an aviary with up to 40 parakeets. We had turtles, fish, tortoises, snakes, rats, hamsters, dogs, fish, lizards. If you can name it, I've most likely had it!

I love animals; they are a part of my everyday life. As an adult, I have the privilege of being called "The Dog Father" with three dogs and two cats. Plans to expand the fur family include a tortoise and chickens as of October 2025. But knowing my wife and I, that probably will not be enough!
